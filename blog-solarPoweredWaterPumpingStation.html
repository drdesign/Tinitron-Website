Solar-powered water pumping stations for farms, ranches, towns and villages

by David Fulton


Tinitron, Inc., a global innovator in power for over 40 years, provides design, engineering, integration and turn-key installation of solar-powered water pumping satiations for farms, ranches, towns and villages in most environments and geographic locations.

Alternate Power: in addition to solar power, Tinitron offers you more options such as 1) wind power, 2) generator, and 3) battery backup for free-flowing water anytime, day or night. Shown below are two typical solar water pumping installations (one pressurized and one unpressurized).

Pressurized Water Delivery System

Site description: This typical solar-powered water wells is set up with a 30 gal. pressure tank and pressure switch. This system delivers water into a filter system at a building over one (1) mile away supplying the building with fresh, clean, usable water at a remote location.

Technical Specifications

Submersible Pump: Tinitron part #860-1000-00 Helical Rotor Type
Solar Panels: Qty 3- 175 Watt Solar Panels.
System Pressure: Approximately 55 psi.
Pole Mounted System: 4 ½ inch Diameter pole.

The Solar Panels are powering the submersible pump and pressure tank. Tinitron provides balance of system and partners with local installers to finish the installation.
