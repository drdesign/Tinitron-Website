High Voltage Transformers
Product Description

Tinitron's high voltage transformers can be found in everything from x-ray equipment used for Non Destructive Testing, to laser power supplies and everything in-between. We understand the unique needs of these transformers, our careful design and  attention to detail ensure that your high voltage transformer will give many years of service without failure.
